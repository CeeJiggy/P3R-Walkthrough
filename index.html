PERSONA 3 RELOAD 100% WALKTHROUGH

Original guide by Monomaru on powerpyx.com

Welcome to the Persona 3 Reload 100% Walkthrough. This 100% Schedule Guide includes all Trophies, Social Links, Linked Episodes, Teammate Dorm Events, and all of the Computer Software. This guide will also allow you to romance and go on a shrine date with whoever you like. Romance routes are written in Pink. Be sure to Romance at least one girl to unlock That Special Someone trophy/achievement.

While there isn’t a trophy for completing all Dorm Events, you must do the Dorm Events for Fuuka and Yukari in order to earn points towards their social links, and earn the trophies Dorm Life , Eat Your Veggies, Peas! and Through Thick and Thin.

Linked Episodes are not needed for any trophies, however they are still included in the schedule, and you will unlock unique Personas for fusion upon finishing them.

Computer software is completely optional after you have maxed your Social Stats. You will however need to select the Dating Site Note on 12/10 and 12/11.

With all that in mind, if you forgot to bring a corresponding Arcana on one of the days, you will be behind on a Social Link. This can be fixed on days where the only task is listed as an optional one. You can also sacrifice days where a Linked Episode is on the schedule, but you will miss out on the unique Persona Fusions.
